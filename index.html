Evaluation Questions

  • What contact variables are measured directly, and what variables are inferred?
  • Does the system report pressure, shear, deformation, slip, or only binary contact?
  • What sampling rate and latency are available at the controller boundary?
  • How is calibration handled across temperature, wear, mounting variation, and repeated contact?
  • Can the data be converted into stable features for learning systems?
  • Does tactile feedback improve a physical task such as grasping, locomotion, inspection, or human-robot interaction?
